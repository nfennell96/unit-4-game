Crystal Collector

How to Play


  1. The computer will pick a random number at the start of the game, you win the game if you guess the number.
  2. There are four crystals:
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. By clicking a crystal, a certain amount of points will be added to your score.
  4. You win the game by matching your score with the random number.
  5. You lose if your score exceeds the random number.
  6. You need to discover the value of each crystal by clicking on them
  7. At the start of each game, the value of the crystals will change.
  8. Good luck!
